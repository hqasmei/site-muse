Introducing SiteMuse – an open-source tool for developers and designers to manage bookmarks and streamline research and inspiration.

## Organize by projects

In addition to fast bookmark management, SiteMuse also offers essential data visualization tools to assist development and design teams in comprehending their bookmarks effortlessly.

![Dashboard page for projects on SiteMuse](https://res.cloudinary.com/duud9d8dv/image/upload/v1694494048/Screenshot_2023-09-11_at_9.47.01_PM_jllxtb.png)

## Add your bookmarks

SiteMuse also allows you to add whatever bookmarks. This feature is particularly beneficial for development and design teams aiming to use their branded domains for bookmark management.

![Use your own bookmarks on SiteMuse](https://res.cloudinary.com/duud9d8dv/image/upload/v1694494048/Screenshot_2023-09-11_at_9.46.53_PM_ygdzlk.png)

## Fully open-source and self-hostable

SiteMuse is entirely open-source, released under the AGPLv3 license.

This means you have the freedom to clone, modify, and even deploy your instance of SiteMuse, as long as you keep it open-source.

Here's a link to the GitHub repository: [https://github.com/hqasmei/site-muse](https://github.com/hqasmei/site-muse)
